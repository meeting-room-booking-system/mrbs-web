Testimonials

Here's some testimonials to show just how great MRBS is!

Dr Dan Swan, Bioinformatician and Systems Administrator for the Barts and the London Genome Centre

The Genome Centre at Queen Mary and Westfield College is a central resource for the scientists of the University. As our equipment is 'common' use, we have implemented MRBS as an 'Equipment Booking System' in order to ensure that there is no confusion about who is entitled to use equipment at any one time.

MRBS is essential to our day to day running and has greatly improved the efficiency of our Centre. Installation was simple and requires no administrative maintenance on my part. Ease of use is paramount, and everyone has got to grips with using MRBS rapidly and comfortably.

User feedback is Very Positive. We don't have a online booking system prior to MRBS. So, to them, this is a big improvement. By and large, since running eight months ago, we have achieved 2420 bookings, totaling 11K+ hours by 156 users. Can't be more successful than that!
